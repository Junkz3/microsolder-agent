Symptom: Internal display flickers intermittently or goes blank after reassembly; HDMI output unaffected. Likely cause: Damaged or mis-seated J24 MIPI-DSI flex cable (30-pin, 0.5 mm pitch) between CPU module and SN65DSI86 eDP bridge, or Wi-Fi antenna routing interference into display path. Components mentioned: J24, U10 (SN65DSI86). Rail / test point: n/a (signal integrity issue, not a rail fault). Under microscope inspect J24 ZIF pads for lifted contacts; re-seat or re-flow.
